Hi! I’m Khazenah, also known as Zenah, and I am an aspiring UX Designer. Throughout my journey to becoming one, I have gained extensive knowledge in the essential elements of web design and development, particularly in effectively utilizing HTML and CSS. At present, I am devoted to mastering the intricacies of JavaScript through Correlation One’s Software Development program, which will empower me to seamlessly integrate it into my code.

I have extensively utilized Figma to craft digital wire-frames, and I am currently making remarkable progress in completing Google’s Professional UX Design certification. Through this certification, I have acquired valuable expertise in conducting competitive audits, immersive story-boarding, user research, prototyping, and various other UX design techniques. I possess a deep understanding of the importance of creating accessible sites, apps, and products for all users. I am thrilled about the prospect of collaborating with a company that is dedicated to developing exceptional human-centered products and websites.
