A Mini Bio About Me

Welcome to a mini bio about me! I am a proud mother of three and a Kansas City, Missouri native, fueled by my deep fascination for all things art. My biggest dream is to establish a thriving business centered around my artistic passions. But that's just the beginning! I also dream of using my acquired skills to empower and inspire underprivileged youth in my community, opening doors to opportunities they may have never thought possible. These goals may seem ambitious, but with unwavering determination, hard work, and a positive mindset, I firmly believe anything is within reach!

I am currently engrossed in pursuing certifications in User Experience design and software engineering. These two fields perfectly harmonize with my inherent creativity. The joy I derive from acquiring newfound knowledge is boundless, and I am excited to further enhance my skills even after completing my current classes.

When I'm not designing amazing apps and websites, I indulge in my passions for drawing, writing, and spending quality time with my loved ones. I also enjoy exploring nature, whether it's by a tranquil lakeside or a picturesque park. I firmly believe in making every moment count and embracing all that life has to offer. Time is a precious gift, and I strive to make the most of it in every situation, cherishing each opportunity that comes my way.

I am very down to earth and family-oriented. I enjoy spending time with my family whenever I am not drawing , writing or designing something. I am also a huge reader and coffee enthusiast!
